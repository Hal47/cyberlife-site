The following is the original list I had on here. I'm leaving it here for the blurbs I wrote up, and the links.


Vaporwave

One of the more interesting genres of music that could be considered cyberpunk is Vaporwave. Interesting, usually slow beats and melodies that riff on elevator music and mall music. Sometimes with lyrics. Parodies corporate music.
    Some good vaporwave:
  • VentureX - Slow Jam II
  • Dystopian Chic - Friday Shopping

Neo-80s

A favorite of /v/ (at least, when I first made the site), this genre has sprung up in the wake of games like Hotline Miami and Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon and movies like Drive and Kung Fury. It's like 80s music, made mostly with modern hardware, and more over-the-top. Well-known artists include Perturbator and Lazerhawk. Lots to be found at NewRetroWave.
    Good neo-80s:
  • Hotline Miami Soundtrack
  • Kavinsky - Nightcall
  • ActRazer - Drift
  • Bourgeoisie - Candy At Plain Sight
  • Carpenter Brut † Teaser EP II † Black BVS Records † Deka Brothers
  • Com Truise - Sundriped
  • LazerHawk - Electric Groove

Industrial

A staple of cyberpunk music. I'm not terribly familiar with the genre, but /cyber/ recommends Front Line Assembly. Even though it's EBM (I think), I'm throwing Front 242 in here.
  • Front Line Assembly - Mindphaser 12" Mix
  • Front Line Assembly - Tactical Neural Implant (album)
  • Front Line Assembly - Synthetic Forms
  • Front 242 - Headhunter
